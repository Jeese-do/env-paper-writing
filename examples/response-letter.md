# 示例：用 env-response-letter 撰写审稿回复

## 输入（用户提供）

> 审稿意见 1（中文）：吸附等温线只用了 Langmuir 模型，建议补充 Freundlich 模型对比。
> 修改说明：已补充 Freundlich 拟合，R²=0.91，低于 Langmuir 的 0.99，说明以单层吸附为主。结果加入表 2 和正文第 4.2 节。
>
> 审稿意见 2（中文）：缺少对实际废水的适用性验证。
> 修改说明：受样品获取限制暂无法补实验，已在讨论中说明局限并提出后续计划。

## 输出（Skill 产出，节选）

**Part 1 [Response Letter]**

```
We thank the editor and the reviewers for their constructive comments, which
have helped us to improve the quality of the manuscript. Our point-by-point
responses are provided below, and all changes have been marked in the revised
manuscript.

Reviewer #1

Comment 1: The adsorption isotherm was fitted only with the Langmuir model;
the authors are advised to include the Freundlich model for comparison.

Response: We agree with the reviewer and have added the Freundlich model for
comparison. The Freundlich fit yielded an R-squared of 0.91, which is lower than
that of the Langmuir model (0.99), indicating that monolayer adsorption is the
dominant process.

Changes: The comparison has been added to Table 2 and described in Section 4.2,
page 7, lines 168-175.

Comment 2: The applicability of the adsorbent to real wastewater was not verified.

Response: We appreciate this valuable suggestion. Owing to current limitations in
sample availability, additional experiments with real wastewater could not be
completed within this revision. We have explicitly acknowledged this limitation
and outlined it as a direction for future work.

Changes: A statement on this limitation has been added to the Discussion,
page 10, lines 245-251.

We thank the reviewers again for their time and look forward to your decision.
```

**Part 2 [中文说明]**

- Comment 1 采纳并给出具体数据与改动位置，回应充分。
- Comment 2 属于「无法补实验」的情形——示范了**礼貌承认局限 + 说明原因 + 提出后续计划**的标准策略，而非回避。
- 待补：实际页码行号请按你的修回稿替换占位（已给示意值）。
