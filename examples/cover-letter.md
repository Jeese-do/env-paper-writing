# 示例：用 env-cover-letter 撰写投稿信

## 输入（用户提供）

> 论文标题：Nitrogen-doped biochar for efficient removal of Cd(II) from contaminated water
> 核心贡献：制备氮掺杂生物炭，对 Cd(II) 去除率 92.3%，最大吸附量 48.6 mg/g，较未改性提升 50%；提出表面络合主导机理。
> 目标期刊：Journal of Hazardous Materials
> 通讯作者：Dr. Lei Wang, School of Environment, XX University, wang@xx.edu.cn

## 输出（Skill 产出，节选）

**Part 1 [Cover Letter]**

```
Dear Editor,

We are pleased to submit our manuscript entitled "Nitrogen-doped biochar for
efficient removal of Cd(II) from contaminated water" for consideration as a
research article in the Journal of Hazardous Materials.

Heavy-metal contamination of water poses a persistent threat to ecosystems and
public health, and the development of low-cost, high-capacity adsorbents remains
a pressing challenge. In this study, we prepared a nitrogen-doped biochar and
systematically evaluated its performance for Cd(II) removal. The modified biochar
achieved a removal efficiency of 92.3% and a maximum adsorption capacity of
48.6 mg g-1, representing an improvement of more than 50% over the pristine
material. Mechanistic analysis indicated that surface complexation governed the
uptake of Cd(II), providing design guidance for engineered biochar adsorbents.

We believe these findings are well suited to the readership of the Journal of
Hazardous Materials, given its focus on the remediation of hazardous pollutants.

This manuscript is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not under
consideration by any other journal. All authors have approved the submission and
declare no conflict of interest.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
Dr. Lei Wang
School of Environment, XX University
wang@xx.edu.cn
```

**Part 2 [中文说明]**

- 第二段突出**定量结果 + 相对提升 + 机理**三要素，让主编一眼看到贡献与新颖性。
- 第三段点明与期刊读者群的契合度。
- 已包含原创性、无一稿多投、作者同意、无利益冲突等常规声明。
- 待补：主编姓名（如已知可替换 `Dear Editor,`）、推荐/回避审稿人（如需）。
